Liam Carroll
bio
Liam Carroll (Berlin) is a freelance designer and web developer from Aotearoa (New Zealand). He holds a BDes (Honours) in spatial design and a GDip Arts in philosophy. In July 2022 he completed the 6 month Full Stack Web Developer program at Career Foundry and is actively taking on projects / work that build upon the skills he learnt there. Ongoing interests include philosophy studies, sound design / production, music curation, and spending time in nature.
